Non-Customer Management System

After an appointment is invoiced, it can only be viewed, not edited or deleted.

Customers can register, log in, and manage their profile. They can book appointments and edit or delete only current bookings‚Äînot past ones. Once an appointment is invoiced, it becomes view-only, and the invoice appears in their profile.

Approved shop owners receive login details and can manage their shop info, services, appointments, and invoices. They can book appointments for customers, add service charges, and send or download invoices as PDF. After invoicing, appointments become view-only, and only invoice cost/payment updates are allowed.

Super Admin Dashboard

Super Admin can manage all shops‚Äîadd, edit, or delete shop owners‚Äîand view system insights such as total appointments, top services, top-earning shops, invoice counts, payment status, and non-customer bookings.
